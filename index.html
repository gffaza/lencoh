Desa Lencoh



Lencoh is a village located in Selo sub-district, Boyolali, Central Java, Indonesia.

Potential tourism is tourism that has an attraction that can attract people to visit these tourist attractions. The attraction can be in the form of natural conditions around tourist attractions as well as existing infrastructure facilities that can provide comfort to visitors so that they feel at home for long in these tourist attractions. Based on Law No. 10 of 2009 the definition of tourist attraction is anything that has uniqueness, beauty, and value in the form of a diversity of natural, cultural, and man-made wealth that is the target or purpose of tourist visits.
Desa Lencoh

Natural Tourist Attraction

Natural tourism attractions are sourced from existing natural conditions including proximity to the natural surroundings or the environment such as beach tourism, marine tourism, mountainous nature tourism, wild and remote area tours, park tours and conservation areas. For Lencoh Village itself, the scenery presented is Mount Merapi, Mount Merbabu and Mount Sindoro and Sumbing.

Tourist Attractions in Lencoh Village

Mount Nganten Tourism in Lencoh Village

It is named Lencoh because it is located in Lencoh Village, Selo District, Boyolali Regency, Central Java. Located on the edge of the road, and is the right spot to enjoy the beauty and splendor of Mount Merapi and Mount Merbabu. This area is indeed a point that is considered the most suitable for taking photos. Especially when the weather is sunny, Mount Merapi and Merbabu look beautiful. While in the afternoon tourists can clearly see the sunset on the western horizon. It is a tourist village managed by the Lencoh Village-Owned Enterprise. Interestingly again, visitors or tourists can camp by setting up a tent, with a rental fee of Rp. 40,000, - up to Rp. 60,000, - provided by the manager. You can imagine how serene it is, camping in the open with the beautiful roof of the stars and shrouded in fog and the cold night on the mountain. If visitors feel hungry, the tourist village of Lencoh also provides meal packages, however, many of the visitors choose to bring their own lunch or food ingredients and cook at the tourist site with their own utensils.
